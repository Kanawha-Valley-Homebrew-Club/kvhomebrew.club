Benefits of club membership include…

  • Club meetings the last Thursday of every month
  • Organized group brew days
  • Group Trips & Gatherings
  • Fellowship and guidance of other brewers
  • Discounted group grain buys
  • Membership card with discounts at partner retailers
  • Used equipment sales
  • Expert guest speakers from the lands of yeast, hops, and brewing science!
  • Fun club competitions
  • Local ingredients (yeast banks, foraged adjuncts, hop giveaways)
